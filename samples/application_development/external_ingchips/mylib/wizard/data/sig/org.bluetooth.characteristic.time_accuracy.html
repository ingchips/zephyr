Time Accuracy

org.bluetooth.characteristic.time_accuracy

Value
NameRequirementFormatNote
Accuracy

Accuracy (drift) of time information in steps of 1/8 of a second (125ms) compared to a reference time source. Valid range from 0 to 253 (0s to 31.5s). A value of 254 means Accuracy is out of range (> 31.5s). A value of 255 means Accuracy is unknown.		Mandatoryuint8
