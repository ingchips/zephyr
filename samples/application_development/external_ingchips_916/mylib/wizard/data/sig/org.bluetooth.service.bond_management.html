Bond Management Service

org.bluetooth.service.bond_management

Abstract
This Specification proposes that this service will enable users to manage their bonds on devices with a limited user interface.
Summary
This service defines how a peer Bluetooth device can manage the storage of bond information, especially the deletion of it, on the Bluetooth device supporting this service.
Note
C1: Mandatory if operand longer than MTU is requested, else optional
